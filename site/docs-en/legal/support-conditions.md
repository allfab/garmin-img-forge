## 1. Nature of the support

The "Fund a summit" button proposes to make a **free donation in favor of the GARMIN IMG FORGE project**. This is not a purchase of goods or services: the donor receives no direct consideration (no premium access, no exclusive feature, no delivery).

The minimum amount is **€4**. The donor freely chooses the amount they wish to contribute.

---

## 2. Payment processing

| Step | Responsible |
|-------|-------------|
| Collection of banking data | LemonSqueezy |
| Calculation and collection of VAT | LemonSqueezy (according to donor's country) |
| Issuance of receipt / invoice | LemonSqueezy |
| Retention of payment data | LemonSqueezy |

The transaction takes place on LemonSqueezy's infrastructure, secured via HTTPS and PCI-DSS compliant.

## 3. Use of funds

Donations received contribute to costs incurred by the author to maintain the project:

- Server infrastructure costs (hosting, storage)
- Test equipment (Garmin GPS devices)
- Service fees used by the pipeline (bandwidth, etc.)

There is no guaranteed allocation of donations to a specific use.

---

## 4. Right of withdrawal

In accordance with Article L.221-28 of the French Consumer Code, **the 14-day right of withdrawal** applicable to online purchases **does not apply to voluntary donations** made without consideration.

Nevertheless, LemonSqueezy may process refund requests on a case-by-case basis within a reasonable time following the transaction. For any request, contact LemonSqueezy support directly or the publisher at **allfab@gmail.com**.

---

## 5. Taxation

Donations made via this site are **not tax-deductible** (this is not a donation to a recognized public interest organization).

The applicable VAT, where applicable, is calculated and managed by LemonSqueezy in accordance with the legislation in force in the donor's country.
